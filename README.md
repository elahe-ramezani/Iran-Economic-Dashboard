# Iran Economic Dashboard | Power BI

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Iran's economic position relative to neighboring countries from 2020 to 2024.

## Project Overview

This project evaluates Iran's relative economic position using Nominal GDP (Current USD) as the primary economic size indicator, combined with GDP growth, inflation, GDP per capita, regional rankings, GDP gap analysis, and economic volatility measures.

## Key Questions

- What is Iran's economic position relative to neighboring countries?
- How has Iran's regional GDP ranking changed over time?
- How large is the GDP gap between Iran and the regional leader?
- Is the gap widening or narrowing?
- How volatile are GDP growth and inflation?
- How does Iran compare geographically with neighboring economies?

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Relative Economic Position
Overview of Iran's current position compared with neighboring economies.

### 2. Ranking Dynamics
Analysis of Iran's GDP ranking and economic performance trends over time.

### 3. Gap Analysis
Analysis of Iran's GDP gap relative to the regional leader and regional median.

### 4. Volatility & Fluctuation
Analysis of GDP growth volatility and inflation volatility.

### 5. Map & Relative Strength
Geographic comparison of economic size and relative regional strength.

### 6. Executive Economic Summary
Final comparison of key economic indicators across neighboring economies.

## Key Findings

- Iran remained among the top regional economies by nominal GDP.
- Iran maintained the second position in the selected regional comparison.
- The GDP gap with the regional leader widened during the later years of the analysis period.
- Inflation volatility exceeded GDP growth volatility.
- Iran remained one of the largest economies in the analyzed region.
